S1m0ne ft. Pam

What if we replaced actors with computers? A thought provoking question that we’re still grappling with the answer to 20 years later.

In 2002, S1m0ne posed the question of what would happen if actors were to be replaced with computers. What 20 years ago seemed like a fantasy in the realm of science fiction has increasingly begun to rear its head in our world, and we as a society are still grappling with the consequences and implications of our increasingly virtual reality.

Computer-generated actors, models, influencers, and pop stars, we’ve got them all on this week’s episode.

Hosted by Liam, Abram, and Pam.
